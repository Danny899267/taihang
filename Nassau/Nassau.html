What can we do for the Nassau Botanical Garden?

Taihang Environmental Art Fountain Co., Ltd., our professional areas include fountain, lighting systems and acoustic engineering, etc.

1. Musical Fountain

Large musical fountain

Musical fountain includes: creativity, design, laser, water curtain movie, lighting

Miami International Music Festival

Our company built a spectacular musical fountain for the Miami International Music Festival

2. Dry Fountain

Large square dry fountain

When not spraying water, the ground is flat and open, which can be used as a pedestrian walkway or a place for leisure activities, making full use of the space, especially suitable for areas with large flow of people such as squares

Small square dry fountain

The dry fountain combines waterscape and lighting effects. Through carefully choreographed music and lighting design, synchronized with the changes in the fountain water column, it creates a multi-level audio-visual experience with high viewing value.

3. Interactive Fountain

Graffiti interactive fountain

Participants can draw any pattern on the screen, and the falling water curtain of the fountain will transform into the pattern of graffiti on the screen

Swing interactive fountain

The person swinging on the swing needs to judge the best time to swing according to the time when the water curtain falls.

Jumping Fountain

The fountain sprays out an arc-shaped water column that slides through the air. It can be installed in a square or on the roadside. When people walk on the road, the water column crosses over their heads.

Piano Interactive Fountain

There are simulated piano keys on the ground. When people step on them, they will make the sound of piano keys. At the same time, the jumping fountain next to it will jump with the music.

Fog Forest Fountain

The mist fountain can sense whether there are people around to spray water mist and lower the ambient temperature.

Bicycle Fountain

When the participants pedal the bicycle, as the bicycle rotates faster, the fountain water column will become higher and higher.

4. Interactive Projection

Interactive Map

By touching the map on the wall, the current regional area will be highlighted in color, delivering a visually striking experience.

Immersive Interactive Projection

Through the projection covering both walls and floor, visitors enter an immersive projection space, experiencing the realism brought by the 3D effect.

Running Interactive Wall

The figures in the projected image can move in sync with people running in front of the screen.

Interactive Projection Corridor

The corridor projection can simulate a sandy beach, leaving footprints as you walk over it. It can also simulate various objects, allowing participants to interact by stepping on them.

5. Light Show

The Dance of Plants and Animals

Decorate the botanical garden with lanterns shaped like plants and animals, complemented by laser lights to enhance the garden's vibrant colors at night.

Starry Sky Walkway

Through starlight lighting design, visitors walking along the forest path find themselves surrounded by countless stars, as if floating in outer space.

Projection Animation

Using trees as projection canvases, the lights blend with the plants to create a visual spectacle for visitors.

Pathway Projection

Using projectors, pre-designed patterns are cast onto the walkways from above, replacing traditional lighting to make the botanical garden's nights more vibrant.
